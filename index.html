04 // SPECIFICATIONS

Material Integrity & Object Aftercare

Every piece is a living structure. Review our seasonal care guides to preserve the organic surface tension of your object.

01 / Solid Timber Hardwoods

Treat every six months with pure Swedish linseed oil mixture. Avoid prolonged direct afternoon sunlight exposure to avoid natural hue shifting over centuries of display.

02 / Raw Honed Travertine

Our stone surfaces are intentionally left unsealed to highlight their natural ancient history. Wipe away acidic compounds immediately using lukewarm structured spring water.

03 / Hand-Thrown Clay Ceramics

Fired slowly over volcanic basalt coal matrices. Clean gently with structural microfiber items without adding rough chemical scouring liquids.

05 // ETHOS

Slowing down the rhythm of mass manufacturing.

We work exclusively with multi-generational workshops in Denmark and Japan. By minimizing machine processing, we preserve the organic textures and unique natural imperfections of the raw materials.
